Eduwaves Publishers Limited

We are an emergent Publishing house with interest in the publication of core curriculum text books. We also publish memoirs, autobiographies, inaugurals, academic journals, etc.

We are stockists for Universities Libraries, Polytechnics, and CoEs We also undertake various other roles in the educational ecosystem, e.g Exercise books projects, Science and laboratory equipments, flash cards, eLibrary Development Services, Wall charts for schools, etc
